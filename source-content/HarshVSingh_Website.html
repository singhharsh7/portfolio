About Harsh V Singh

Harsh V Singh spent his twenties chasing chief ministers for a quote and hospital corridors for a story. Today he runs global project delivery for Rang Digitech LLC, as Associate Director - Project Delivery, based out of Piscataway, New Jersey, but running his team day-to-day from Rang's Vadodara and Ahmedabad offices in Gujarat, leading global delivery for a cross-functional team that manages client relationships and campaign execution across the US, UK, Canada, UAE, and India. He stepped into the role in April 2026, after two years climbing through content leadership and brand management at Rang Digitech and Rang Technologies.

Before any of that, he was a reporter — bylined stories for The Times of India, the International Business Times (Singapore Edition), NewsX, and British Herald, reporting that took him from hospital wards in Hyderabad to the floor of the Karnataka Vidhan Soudha, sitting across from sitting and former Chief Ministers, party spokespeople, and civil servants to get the quote that mattered. That grounding, verify the fact, hit the deadline, say the complicated thing simply, still drives how he thinks about brand strategy and corporate communications now. Just aimed at a different kind of story.

What he does now

Harsh owns end-to-end delivery for a portfolio of active accounts spanning digital marketing services at Rang Digitech, directing operations across several client portfolios and geographies at once, allocating resources and coordinating teams through concurrent engagements, keeping a close eye on the KPIs that actually indicate whether work is landing, and protecting brand compliance and online reputation for the accounts in his care. What he's really doing, though, is the same thing he did as a reporter: sitting at the intersection of storytelling, SEO, and applied AI, using all three to build campaigns that move real numbers instead of vanity metrics.

How he got here

His run at Rang Digitech and Rang Technologies (2023–present) started as Senior Content Writer and Head of the Content Department, leading a team of SEO, social media, and design professionals across eight-plus companies inside the group, before moving into Brand Manager and then his current role.

Before Rang, he wrote for GoodFirms under OpenXcell, content across software development, mobile app development, SEO, blockchain, and digital marketing for a global client base, published under the pen name Ryan Allen. As Manager of Creative Content and Social Media at Kanan.Co, he led content curation for Visa Crunch in the study-abroad and EdTech space, directed academic content for KananPrep and its franchises, and ran campaigns that lifted brand engagement by 40 percent. Before that, PR at Jindal Saw Limited, coordinating with 15-plus commercial function departments on company literature, CSR campaigns, and internal communications; and, in parallel, Brifly News, a Bangalore-based news venture he co-founded, where as Editorial Head he curated news into 60-word summaries and trained a team of writers on SEO-friendly reporting.

And before all of that: journalism proper. At the International Business Times (Singapore Edition), he covered the Hollywood beat out of Bangalore. At The Times of India in Hyderabad, on the civic affairs and public health beat, he racked up 13 published bylines in 7 days, including the hospital sanitation investigation, a report on organ donation shortfalls, road safety coverage on Necklace Road, and a story on political messaging in government school notebooks. At NewsX's Bangalore bureau, covering Karnataka state politics, he ran camera and OB van operations and landed on-camera interviews with Chief Minister Siddaramaiah, former Chief Minister B.S. Yeddyurappa, KPCC president Dr. G. Parameshwara, and JD(S) spokesperson Tanveer Ahmed, reporting live from the Vidhan Soudha during the presidential election proceedings for Ram Nath Kovind. A British Herald piece on Modi winning World's Most Powerful Person went viral in June 2019.

Learning

An MA in Journalism & AVC from COMMITS Bangalore, a BA in Journalism from Presidency College (Promising Journalist Award, 2018), and a B.Com from Garden City College — plus NCC's "A" Certificate and district-level football and cricket along the way. Since then, 125+ certifications in digital marketing, SEO, generative AI, leadership, and brand strategy through HubSpot, Semrush, Google, Reuters, and LinkedIn Learning, alongside 600+ books, 150+ documentaries, and 600+ films.

What he writes

Nine-plus years in, journalism and brand strategy still run on the same fuel: get the facts right, then make people care. Harsh has interviewed sitting chief ministers, a former prime minister, Indian ambassadors, actors, singers, foreign MPs, Indian MLAs, MLCs, and MPs, RTI commissioners, scholars, and Nobel laureate Kailash Satyarthi — and broken investigative stories that prompted government scrutiny. He now leads project delivery for a digital agency's global client base spanning four continents, working out of Vadodara and Ahmedabad, Gujarat.
